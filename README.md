## Overview

Welcome to the repository for the official website of **The Shield LY**, a leading technology and security company based in Benghazi, Libya. We specialize in providing integrated solutions that blend protection with advanced technology, ensuring quality and trust in every project. Our services include:

- **Surveillance Systems**: Installation and maintenance of high-quality security cameras (#كاميرات_مراقبة).
- **Electrical Networks**: Design and implementation of robust electrical infrastructure (#شبكات_كهربائية).
- **Large-Screen Installations**: Expert setup, supply, and servicing of giant indoor screens for institutions and events.
- **Lighting and Infrastructure**: Custom solutions for exhibitions, pavilions, and critical sectors like traffic management (#مرور_بنغازي).
- **Technical Solutions**: Comprehensive tech integrations for businesses and public projects in Libya (#حلول_تقنية #ليبيا).

This website acts as an online extension of our Facebook page[](https://www.facebook.com/TheShieldLY), offering detailed information on our mission, services, and recent projects, such as our contributions to the Libya Build exhibition.

## Mission

At The Shield, we translate expertise into trust. Our goal is to deliver innovative, reliable tech solutions that enhance security and efficiency, meeting the unique needs of our clients in Libya and beyond.

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Built with modern web technologies for seamless viewing on desktops, tablets, and mobiles.
- **Service Pages**: Dedicated sections for each service with descriptions, case studies, and contact forms.
- **Project Gallery**: Highlights of past work, including videos and images from events like the Alam Al Jibal pavilion at Libya Build.
- **Contact Integration**: Easy ways to reach us via messaging or phone (0915564024).
